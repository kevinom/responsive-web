Vancouver, Summer 2016

Abstract — This webpage describes my brother's trip to Vancouver in the summer of 2016. The page is an example of a responsive web page that automatically sizes according to the dimensions of the display.

Tuesday 9 August 2016

Terry arrives on Cathay Pacific after a gruelling 34 hour trip via Hong Kong. Clouds right down to the runway. Gail and Kevin park next to the runway and see the plane land.

Terry comes to watch Kevin play Tuesday night doubles and falls asleep watching some of the Olympics on the club's TV.

I don't blame him!

Friday 12 August 2016

Terry cycles to the Seymour Demonstration Forest 13 km each way.

Go and pick up the other bike at Martin's place.

Terry and Kevin take the bikes to ride on the dykes on the side of the Pitt River. Stop at the Pitt Meadows GC to have a post-game beer, even though we didn't play.

Saturday 13 August 2016

We go on the BC Ferry to Vancouver Island. Drive to the Tssawwassen Ferry terminal and walk on.

Tom picks us up on the Vancouver Island side and takes us to see the Law Facility at U. Vic.

Lunch at an Italian restaurant in downtown Victoria for Tom's birthday (12 August) joined by David and Jenny and family.

Tom takes us on a drive around Oak Bay and Uplands suburbs of Victoria. Terry is amazed at the properties.

Ride home on the 6PM ferry and Isla is very sweet.

Sunday 14 August 2016

Time to say goodbye!
